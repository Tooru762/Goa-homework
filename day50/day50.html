Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal.

Preparation time:

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, meats

Instructions:

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper until well mixed.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Pour in the eggs and cook evenly.
  4. Add fillings if desired, fold, and cook for another minute.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Nutrition:

  • Calories: 277 kcal
  • Protein: 20g
  • Carbs: 0g
  • Fat: 22g
